What is Nintendo?

Nintendo is a game making, electronics, and software company from Japan that sells its products in many countries. This company was originally a business that made playing cards, but it has since evolved into the video game company responsible for popular game franchises such as Mario and Pokemon. Its consoles and handheld devices, such as the Nintendo Game Boy and the Nintendo Wii, have been a hit among young and old, male and female audiences. With the creation of Pokemon GO marking Nintendo's start to making mobile games and a new console on the way in March 2017, Nintendo always gives its fans something to look forward to!
